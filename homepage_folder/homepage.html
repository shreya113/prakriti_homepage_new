What is MINIGURU


We at MINIGURU work to provide a platform to,the Future Leader,to keep their syllabus aside for sometime and to adore their Creativeness.

Children are naturally creative,it is our job to give them the freedom and space to let their creativity blossom to its fullest.

We provide the best possible experience with facilities like earning coins ,appreciation, merits to bring out the best in them.We have mentors to assist them so they can fill their palette of imagination with every color of fun and happiness.

We also provide separate sections to draw and upload their projects so that children learn the importance of systematic work.We work for their happiness and development in every way possible.
