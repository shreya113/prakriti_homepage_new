What is MINIGURU


Children are naturally curious and creative. They have uncanny ability to learn. Unfortunately this is not given sufficient weightage in their education.

Miniguru.in is unique platform, dedicated to children to nurture their inherent creativity. Miniguru aspires to give space to children to learn and grow in an environment of trust, responsibility and freedom. Miniguru is a hub of children’s dream and ideas, encouraging children to execute at their pace and interest.

We help schools and parents to create a physical space for their children; named T-LAB, which consists of different work stations and is a resource repository with a variety of tools, materials and references. T-LAB is a space for ‘doing’. Children decide their own list of things to do and their own way of doing things.

Miniguru.in avail facilities to channelize their project work in a planned and constructive manner. These processes boost children’s confidence and keep their motivation alive. Miniguru gives opportunity to reflect and share their ideas and projects which they receive constructive feedback from their peers.

In other word, it is a clear platform where they learn from their own, and the peer play a role of guru by giving feedback and reflection on their projects. That is why we named it Miniguru, a unique and ever evolving peer learning space.

The miniguru.in is group of MINIGURUs to each other.
